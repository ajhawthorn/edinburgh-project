Edinburgh's top festivals

# Festival Description More information
1 Edinburgh's Fringe Festival (August) The largest arts festival in the world, with a range of shows including dance, theatre, music and comedy. Fringe Festival
2 Edinburgh International Festival (August) A festival of the performing arts including theatre, dance and opera. International Festival
3 Hogmanay (New Year) A huge street party to celebrate the start of the new year. Edinburgh's Hogmanay
4 Edinburgh Jazz and Blues Festival A celebration of jazz and blues music from local and international artists. Jazz and Blues Festival
5 Edinburgh International Book Festival (August) An annual literary festival with author readings, discussions and workshops. Edinburgh International Book Festival
